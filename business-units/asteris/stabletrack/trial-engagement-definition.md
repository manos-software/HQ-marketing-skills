# Engagement Segmentation — StableTrack
> version: 1.0.0

## High engagement
- Created 5+ horses OR
- Generated 1+ invoice OR
- Multiple sessions

## Medium engagement
- Created 1–4 horses
- Some activity but no invoice
- Partial workflow usage
- Hasn't logged in 4 days

## Low engagement
- 0 horses created OR
- Only logged in once
- No meaningful actions completed
