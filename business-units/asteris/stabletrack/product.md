# Product — StableTrack

## Overview
StableTrack is a cloud-based, offline-capable equine practice management platform built for field use.

## Core modules

### AI SOAP documentation
- Voice-to-text AI captures notes during exams
- Automatically formats SOAP notes
- Works offline and syncs later

### Scheduling
- Field visit scheduling
- Recurring appointments
- Route-based workflows
- Client notifications

### Medical records
- Longitudinal patient history
- Treatments, prescriptions, imaging
- Multi-owner and trainer relationships

### Billing and invoicing
- Services flow directly from documentation to invoice
- No separate billing step
- Reduces missed charges

### Inventory
- Track medications and supplies
- Low-stock alerts
- Controlled substance tracking

### Imaging integration
- Native integration with Asteris PACS
- View and manage imaging within patient record

## Key differentiators

### Offline-first
Works fully in barns and rural environments without connectivity.

### Documentation → billing continuity
Eliminates the gap between work performed and charges recorded.

### Equine-specific workflows
Designed for multi-owner horses, trainers, and barn environments.

## Perceived value
- Immediate ROI through recovered revenue
- Time savings from reduced admin work
- Increased accuracy in billing and records
