# StableTrack — Brand Intelligence

## Product summary
StableTrack is an AI-powered equine practice management system designed for ambulatory veterinarians.  
It helps practices capture, document, and bill for services in real time, directly in the field.

## Core value proposition
Revenue recovery.

Equine vets lose revenue through:
- missed charges
- delayed invoicing
- incomplete documentation

StableTrack ensures every service performed is recorded, billed, and invoiced within a single workflow.

## Target audience
- Independent equine veterinarians
- Ambulatory / field-based practices
- Small teams (1–4 vets)
- Operating in rural or low-connectivity environments

## Core pain points
- Missing charges after farm visits
- Doing admin work after hours
- Poor internet connectivity in barns
- Fragmented tools (notes, billing, scheduling)

## Differentiators
- Offline-first architecture (works without internet)
- AI-generated SOAP notes
- Documentation → invoice continuity
- Multi-owner horse management
- Built specifically for equine workflows

## Messaging pillars
1. Revenue recovery (primary)
2. Built for the barn
3. Capture every charge
4. One workflow from exam to invoice

## Tone
- Practical
- Direct
- Field-oriented
- No fluff
- No corporate language

## Words to use
- "In the field"
- "At the barn"
- "Capture every charge"
- "Never miss a billable service"
