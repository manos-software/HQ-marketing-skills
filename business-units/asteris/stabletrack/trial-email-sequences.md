# Trial Email Sequences — StableTrack
> version: 1.0.0

## Purpose
Define all trial email sequences by engagement segment.

This file is the source of truth for:
- email content
- A/B variants
- testing iterations

---

## Global rules

- One email = one action
- Keep under 180 words
- Use equine-specific language
- No generic SaaS tone
- Focus on real workflows (barn, farm calls, billing)
