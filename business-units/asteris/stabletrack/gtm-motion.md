- Demo: "day in the life" scenario, not feature tour

## Strategic narrative

**The story skills should tell:**
Equine practice is in crisis. Half of new graduates leave within five years. Solo vets work 13-hour days, then do paperwork at night. They lose 5–10% of revenue to missed charges — and every day they delay entering an invoice, they lose more. Their software was either built for companion animal clinics or for large hospitals — neither fits.

StableTrack exists because Asteris saw this gap and built the first AI-native PMS for the way equine vets actually work. It works offline. It captures charges in real time. Voice notes become SOAP records. Documentation connects to billing to payment in one workflow.

The result: a practice where the vet leaves the barn knowing everything is documented, billed, and on its way to getting paid. No paperwork at 10pm. No forgotten charges.

**Narrative constraints:**
- Cite sources for revenue numbers (JAVMA 2024, AAHA, AVMA)
- Target all three segments, not just solo
- No competitor names in customer-facing content
- Ground claims in field reality
- Respect professional expertise — never condescend
- AI = "supports workflow" — never "replaces judgment"
