# GTM Motion — StableTrack

## Market context
- ~5,000 practices in core geographies
- Small, tight-knit community
- Word-of-mouth driven
- Low competition in equine-specific PMS

## Strategy principle
Start with high-trust, high-conversion channels before scaling outbound.

## Priority GTM channels

### 1. Asteris customer base (highest priority)
- Existing Keystone users
- Warm, trusted relationships
- Highest conversion potential

### 2. Design partners (first 10 customers)
- High-touch onboarding
- Direct feedback loops
- Source of testimonials and referrals

### 3. Conferences and associations
- AAEP and regional associations
- High-density access to ICP

### 4. Outbound (targeted)
- Highly specific outreach
- Pain-driven messaging
- Offer onboarding support

### 5. Content and inbound
- SEO around equine workflows
- Comparison pages (vs Cassadol, ezyVet)
- Long-term compounding channel

## Conversion levers
- Revenue leakage narrative
- Offline-first capability
- Built specifically for equine vets

## Early-stage focus
- Activation and onboarding
- Feedback loops
- First 10–50 customers
- High-touch engagement

## Not a priority (yet)
- Paid acquisition at scale
- Broad awareness campaigns
- Complex funnel optimization
